# Shock Extracts

This folder contains structured knowledge about transient shock response.

## What belongs here

- Extracted statements on pulse duration, spectral content, SRS interpretation, modal participation, and response metrics
- Notes on local versus global shock response behavior
- Rule candidates for truncation, damping, and convergence under shock loading

## What should not go here

- General modal theory with no shock relevance
- Final rule implementations
- Qualification decisions that are not backed by extracted evidence

## How it fits into the AVA pipeline

These extracts seed shock-specific concepts and rule packs. They are the technical basis for AVA workflows that reason about transient load environments and structural response fidelity.
