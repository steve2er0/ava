# Modal Extracts

This folder contains structured knowledge about modal representation and adequacy.

## What belongs here

- Effective mass rules, participation factors, truncation guidance, and mode selection observations
- Extracted statements on damping representation inside modal models
- Limits and caveats that affect how modal results are interpreted

## What should not go here

- Shock-only decision logic with no modal basis
- Executable rules or workflow prompts
- Unstructured lecture notes

## How it fits into the AVA pipeline

These extracts provide the modal foundation for downstream concepts and rules. They support AVA decisions about model adequacy, retained frequency range, and interpretation of reduced-order dynamic models.
