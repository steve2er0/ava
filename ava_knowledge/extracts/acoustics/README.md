# Acoustics Extracts

This folder contains structured knowledge about acoustic loading and response.

## What belongs here

- Statements on diffuse field loading, panel coupling, cavity response, and acoustic fatigue considerations
- Extracted assumptions behind acoustic boundary conditions and response metrics
- Rule candidates for when acoustic effects should be evaluated alongside vibration

## What should not go here

- Shock-specific notes with no acoustic coupling
- Final workflow logic unrelated to acoustic reasoning
- Broad textbook summaries that do not identify actionable conditions

## How it fits into the AVA pipeline

This branch extends AVA beyond mechanical shock and vibration into acoustic loading. The extracted statements here will eventually support acoustic concepts, rules, and agent workflows.
