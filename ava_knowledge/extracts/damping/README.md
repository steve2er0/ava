# Damping Extracts

This folder contains structured statements about damping assumptions and evidence.

## What belongs here

- Damping ratio guidance, loss factor mappings, test-derived damping observations, and frequency-dependent cautions
- Statements about uncertainty and conservatism in damping selection
- Domain-specific damping limits for shock, vibration, or acoustic response

## What should not go here

- Raw material data sheets without extraction structure
- Final solver settings with no supporting reasoning
- Broad summaries that do not identify applicability limits

## How it fits into the AVA pipeline

These extracts support concept formation and rule authoring where response sensitivity depends strongly on damping assumptions. They are especially important for preventing non-conservative shock predictions.
